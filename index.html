About Me

A few things you need to know

Since my young age, I have been passionate about computers and technologies.
My curiosity to understand what's behind everything led me to start working on embedded systems during my Computer Science studies back in 2012. After a technical degree, I went on to pass an engineering degree in France (Master equivalent). After a year, I paused my studies for 6 months to work on a project called Whisper at that time... Then I worked at Spire as an intern in 2015. It was a wonderful experience to live in the startup ecosystem of San Francisco for 6 months. When I came back in France to complete my degree, Whisper had the new name of Equisense. I fully integrated the team as Firmware developer, while finishing school. There, I developed trackers to analyze horse riding which resulted in 2 products, the first one being the result of a successful Kickstarter campaign. In 2019, I decided to leave the team for new adventures: traveling around the world with my girlfriend. I started working as a Freelance during that trip.

I am now working full-time as a freelance.
