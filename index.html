About Me

A few things you need to know

From a very young age, I have been passionate about computers and technologies.
My curiosity to understand what is behind everything led me to start working on embedded systems during my Computer Science studies back in 2012. After a technical degree, I went on to receive an engineering degree in France (Master equivalent). After a year, I paused my studies for 6 months to help a friend launch his company called Whisper at that time... In 2015, I started working at Spire as an intern. These 6 months in the startup ecosystem of San Francisco have been a wonderful experience. When I came back to France to complete my degree, Whisper had become Equisense. I fully integrated the team as a Firmware developer, while finishing school. In the company, I was responsible for developing trackers to analyze horse riding. Together with the entire team, we launched 2 products, the first one being the result of a successful Kickstarter campaign. In 2019, I decided to leave the team to start on a new adventure: traveling around the world. I started working as a freelancer during that trip.

I am now working full-time as a freelancer.
